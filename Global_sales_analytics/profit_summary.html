Profit Dashboard Overview

Dashboard Objective

This dashboard evaluates profitability performance between 2014 and 2021 across regions, countries, product categories, and sales channels. The objective is to identify the most profitable business segments, measure cost efficiency, and uncover opportunities to improve margins and overall financial performance.

Key Metrics Analyzed

  • Total Profit
  • Profit Margin
  • Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)
  • Profit Contribution by Country
  • Profit Contribution by Product Category
  • Monthly Profit Trends
  • Order Distribution by Priority

Profit Dashboard Highlights

  • The business generated $3.97 Billion in total profit during the analysis period.
  • An overall 42.12% profit margin indicates strong cost efficiency and healthy profitability.
  • Household products generated the highest profit contribution at approximately $724 Million.
  • Cosmetics followed closely with more than $718 Million in profit.
  • Office Supplies ranked third among all product categories, contributing over $521 Million in profit.
  • Profitability remained consistent throughout the analysis period, supported by effective cost management and strong category performance.

Business Value of the Analysis

This analysis helps identify the product categories and markets generating the highest profitability while highlighting areas where cost efficiency can be improved. By comparing profit contribution, margin performance, and cost structure across business segments, decision-makers can focus resources on the most profitable opportunities and improve overall financial performance.

Key Takeaway

The business generated $3.97 Billion in profit with a strong 42.12% profit margin, demonstrating effective cost management and healthy financial performance. Household and Cosmetics emerged as the leading profit drivers, while the overall profit structure indicates significant opportunities for targeted investment in high-performing categories.
